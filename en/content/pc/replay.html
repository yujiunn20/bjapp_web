Replay

Replay reviews saved Game session records with overall statistics, mistake filtering, and a hand-by-hand Timeline. The goal is to identify recurring problems and then return to Play or Training to address them.

Session Statistics

The Replay home page summarizes saved Game records, including:

  • Total wagered, net profit, and ROI
  • Session count, hand count, decision count
  • Win / Loss results
  • Decision accuracy rate
  • Usage count by Mode (Basic, Hi-Lo, Hi-Opt I)
  • Bet mistake, action mistake, and Insurance mistake counts

Mistake Filters

Filter the record list by category to quickly find problem sessions:

  • All: Show every record.
  • Mistakes: Any type of mistake.
  • Bet Mistakes: Sessions where betting didn't match the Bet Ramp setting.
  • Action Mistakes: Sessions where a decision didn't match the strategy.
  • Insurance Mistakes: Sessions where an Insurance judgment was wrong.

Timeline Hand Review

Opening a record enters the detailed Replay view:

  • Summary: Basic results and overall state of the session.
  • Mistakes: All bet, action, or Insurance mistakes in that session listed together for a quick overview.
  • Timeline: Step through the session hand by hand. Selecting a point on the Timeline shows the Table Snapshot at that moment above, and the Selected Event's decision, outcome, and judgment details below.

The purpose of Timeline is to help you trace recurring mistakes through a full session, then target them in Play or Training.
